About

Security and research, built at the intersection.

Vektasafe is a consultancy operating across Web3 security, cybersecurity, financial markets, and web development. We work with protocols, startups, and individuals who need rigorous security thinking and technical execution.

Our work spans smart contract development and auditing -- from building production-grade Solidity contracts to identifying vulnerabilities before they cost money -- infrastructure security research covering the Web2 attack vectors that account for 80% of Web3 losses, and financial markets analysis covering DeFi security and on-chain asset risk.

The most dangerous gaps in any system sit at intersections. We cover all of them.
